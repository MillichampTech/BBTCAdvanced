    Frequently asked

    How is the sleeping arranged?
    Dormitories typically range from 3–7 bunks, with additional staff bedrooms (with basins).
    Do we need to bring bedding?
    Bedding is provided (sheets, duvets, pillows and covers). Blankets are available on request.
    What’s included for training sessions?
    Projector/screen and wipe boards are available, plus flexible table/chair set-ups.
    Is the building accessible?
    Yes — it’s a single-storey building with ramp access and disabled friendly facilities.
    How do leaders manage bed allocations?
    Use the Bedding list page to allocate beds, print a list for arrival, and send the completed list via the contact prefill.
