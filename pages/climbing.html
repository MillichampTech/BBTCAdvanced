Booking rules (important)

  • 28 days notice: bookings require at least 28 days notice (numbers + age range).
  • Insurance: groups must have adequate insurance for hazardous sports.
  • Own instructor: qualifications and paperwork must be provided for inspection by the site warden.
  • Wall rules: all instructors must follow the rules applied to the climbing wall.
