What guides the work of the Centre

Our Mission & Values

Rooted in Christian faith. Committed to safe, purposeful and well-run experiences.

The Centre exists to serve young people and the leaders who guide them. As part of The Boys’ Brigade family, our work reflects its Christian ethos and long tradition of developing character, confidence and community.

We provide a secure, welcoming and structured environment where groups can focus on growth, teamwork and shared experience.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, well-managed and welcoming centre where young people and leaders grow through shared experience, training and purposeful activity, shaped by the values of The Boys’ Brigade and its commitment to service.

We aim to be a place where young people are challenged, supported and encouraged to grow.

Our Values

Safety & Responsibility

A secure and well-managed environment where young people can enjoy activities with confidence.

Respect & Courtesy

A culture of mutual respect, clear boundaries and positive conduct.

Character & Challenge

Activities designed to build resilience, teamwork and confidence.

Learning & Development

Training that strengthens leaders and enhances local programmes.

Stewardship

Responsible care of our buildings, grounds and resources.

Faith & Service

Grounded in Christian values and committed to serving young people well.
