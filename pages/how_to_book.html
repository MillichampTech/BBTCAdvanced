Bookings

How to book

Bookings are managed through the secure BBNW District booking portal. Register an account, check availability, and submit booking requests online.

Check availability & register Prefer to speak to the Warden first?
The booking portal opens in a new tab.
1 Step 1

Check our availability

Visit the booking portal and check availability using the calendar. The next twelve months are shown initially. Select “later” to view additional dates.

2 Step 2

Create an account

When you are ready, register your organisation / group leader account.

3 Step 3

Account approval

New accounts are reviewed and approved by the Centre Warden. Once approved, you can check availability and submit booking requests.

If you prefer, the Warden can also place a booking on your behalf once your account is approved.

4 Step 4

Submit your booking request

Choose your dates from the first night to the last night of your stay. You will receive email updates as the booking progresses.

5 Step 5

Confirm with deposit

When a booking is provisionally accepted, we will email next steps including any required deposit, payment details, and a link to our Terms & Conditions.

Payment of the deposit confirms your booking and indicates acceptance of our Terms & Conditions.
