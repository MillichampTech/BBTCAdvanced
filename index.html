Booking info

Set on the Fylde Coast in the Lancashire countryside with excellent transport links, the Boys’ Brigade North West District Training and Activity Centre is a welcoming place for learning, adventure, and community.
We offer self-catering camping and residential stays, outdoor activities, events, retreats, meetings, school programmes and sports hire.

The Centre works closely with the local community while welcoming regional, national, and international groups, continuing the Christian ethos on which it was founded in 1989.
